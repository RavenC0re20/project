Local University course guide for A level students


This tool will calculate your University Admission Score (UAS)/Rank Points based on your 'A' Level Grades.
It will also show you courses that might want to apply for based on your results and the subjects you offered.
Each university and course is linked to their respective webpages, you can access it by clicking on the university/course name
Note: Grade C is assumed for both General Paper (GP) and Project Work (PW) in determining the IGP shown in brackets for NUS / NTU / SMU
With effect from this year's IGP, the grade profiles also take into account offers that were made under Aptitude Based Admissions (ABA).
